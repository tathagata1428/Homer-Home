HOMER — Your Personal Life OS

What if one app replaced your habit tracker, goal planner, task board, budget ledger, focus timer, password vault, and AI assistant — with zero ads, zero subscriptions, and your data staying on your device?

That's Homer.

────────────────────────
FEATURES
────────────────────────

🏠 DAILY BRIEF
Start every morning with a personalised dashboard: today's weather, breaking news, a market snapshot, and an inspiring quote — all in one glance.

✅ HABIT TRACKER
Track daily, weekday, or weekly habits. Build streaks. Visualise your 90-day completion history. Small wins, compounded.

🎯 LIFE GOALS
Define goals across career, health, finance, relationships, and personal growth. Set milestones, track progress, and watch the neon bars fill up.

📋 KANBAN BOARD
Manage projects visually. Move tasks from idea → in progress → done. Add subtasks, due dates, and priorities. See a portfolio overview at a glance.

💰 LEDGER
Log income and expenses by category. Set monthly budgets and get a real-time balance. Your financial life, simplified.

⏱️ FOCUS TIMER
Pomodoro-style deep work with customisable session lengths. Live Kanban task overview keeps you locked in on what matters most.

🎵 AMBIENT SOUNDS
Built-in ambient soundscapes for deep focus — rain, lo-fi beats, nature, and more. They keep playing in the background while you work.

🤖 JOEY — AI ASSISTANT
Your private AI companion. Ask questions, draft messages, plan your week, or just think out loud. Powered by cutting-edge AI. All conversations stay yours.

🔐 VAULT
Store credentials, secure notes, and private data behind a PIN. Encrypted on-device. No cloud required.

🚗 CAR TRACKER
Log vehicle documents (insurance, ITP, vignette, driver's licence), maintenance history, and fuel records. Get expiry reminders 30 and 7 days in advance — before they sneak up on you.

📓 JOURNAL
Private daily journaling. Capture thoughts, reflections, and memories with optional cloud sync.

📥 INBOX
Lightning-fast capture. Tasks, links, ideas — grab them now, process later.

🔗 MY LINKS
Save and organise links by category. Your personal bookmarks, always with you.

🔔 SMART REMINDERS
Habit alerts. Document expiry warnings. Recurring reminders. Fully managed on-device — no cloud required, no sign-up needed.

🔄 OFFLINE-FIRST SYNC
Your data is always saved locally first. Optional cloud sync keeps everything in sync across your devices, seamlessly and securely.
