Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 15, 2026  |  Last updated: May 15, 2026

Short version: Homer is a personal productivity app. Your data lives on your device first. Optional cloud sync only activates for authenticated users and is never sold or shared with advertisers. We collect only what is necessary to provide the service.

1. Who We Are

Homer is developed and operated by B4IT (b4it.ro). This privacy policy applies to the Homer Android application (available on Google Play) and the Homer web application.

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at: privacy@b4it.ro

2. Information We Collect

On-Device Only Local productivity data

Homer stores your personal productivity content locally on your device using an on-device database (Android Room / browser localStorage). This includes:

  • Habits and habit completion records
  • Journal entries and mood selections
  • Notes and notebooks
  • Expense records and budgets
  • Pomodoro tasks and focus sessions
  • Reminders and calendar events
  • Saved links and bookmarks
  • Kanban board tasks and projects
  • Vault secrets (stored encrypted on device)
  • Car maintenance records (if you use the Car feature)

This data never leaves your device unless you enable optional cloud sync (see Section 3).

Authentication Account information

If you create an account to enable cross-device sync, we collect your email address and an encrypted password hash. Authentication is handled by Supabase (supabase.com), a trusted infrastructure provider.

AI Features Journal AI reflection

When you voluntarily tap "Get AI Reflection" in the Journal screen, your journal entry text is sent to an AI language model via OpenRouter (openrouter.ai) to generate a reflection and mood analysis. This data is transmitted over HTTPS. We do not store your journal text on any server — it is sent once for processing and the response is returned immediately. OpenRouter's processing is subject to their own privacy policy.

Not Collected We do NOT collect

  • Precise or coarse device location
  • Contacts, phone logs, or SMS
  • Photos, camera, or microphone data
  • Device identifiers for advertising (IDFA/GAID)
  • Advertising tracking data of any kind
  • Browsing history
  • Financial account credentials

3. Cloud Sync (Optional)

Homer offers an optional cloud sync feature powered by Supabase. Sync is disabled by default and only activates when you sign in with your account credentials.

When sync is active, your productivity data (habits, notes, journal entries, tasks, etc.) is uploaded to Supabase cloud storage to enable access across multiple devices. This data is:

  • Transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections
  • Stored in Supabase infrastructure (EU or US region depending on your account)
  • Accessible only by your account (row-level security enforced)
  • Not sold, rented, or shared with third parties

You can delete your cloud data at any time by deleting your account or by contacting us at privacy@b4it.ro.

4. Push Notifications

Homer can send push notifications for:

  • Reminders you explicitly create
  • Habit daily check-in alerts you configure
  • Car maintenance or document expiry alerts (if you use the Car feature)

Notifications are processed entirely on your device using Android's alarm and notification system. No notification content is sent to our servers. You can disable notifications at any time in your device's system settings or within the app.

5. Internet Permissions

The Homer app requests internet access for the following purposes:

  • Optional Supabase cloud sync (when signed in)
  • AI journal reflection (when you tap "Get AI Reflection")
  • Weather data on the Home screen (your approximate city is used, not GPS coordinates)
  • News feed on the News screen
  • Ambient sound streaming (if you use the Ambient Sounds feature)
  • Market/investing data on the Investing screen

6. Third-Party Services

Supabase — Cloud database and authentication provider. Used for optional sync and account management. Privacy policy: supabase.com/privacy

OpenRouter / AI providers — Used for AI journal reflection when you explicitly request it. Your journal text is sent for one-time processing. Privacy policy: openrouter.ai/privacy

Open-Meteo — Weather data provider. We send your city name (not GPS coordinates) to retrieve weather forecasts. No personal data is retained by this service. Privacy policy: open-meteo.com/en/terms

Homer does not use any advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs (Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, etc.), crash reporting SDKs that collect personal data, or social login SDKs.

7. Data Retention

  • On-device data — Retained until you delete it within the app or uninstall the app.
  • Cloud sync data — Retained in Supabase until you delete your account or request deletion.
  • AI reflection requests — Not retained on our servers. The AI provider (OpenRouter) processes and discards per their policy.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your country of residence, you may have the right to:

  • Access — Request a copy of data we hold about you
  • Deletion — Request erasure of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Correction — Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Portability — Request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Withdraw consent — Disable cloud sync or delete your account at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@b4it.ro. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Children's Privacy

Homer is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or 16 in the EU). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, contact us at privacy@b4it.ro and we will promptly delete it.

10. Data Security

We implement the following security measures:

  • All data transmitted to/from servers uses TLS 1.2+ encryption
  • Supabase enforces row-level security — users can only access their own data
  • Vault secrets are stored with additional encryption on-device
  • We do not log or store journal entries or other productivity content on our servers

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. When we make significant changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top and, if the changes are material, notify you through the app or by email. Continued use of Homer after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
